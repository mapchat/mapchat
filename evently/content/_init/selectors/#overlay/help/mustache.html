Greetings!

MapChat is a place for conversations with friends, planning meetings, video and audio sharing and discussions of events.

Most of available actions are put on toolbar:

  • - Sign up / Sign in
  • - Log out
  • - Create/Continue conversation
  • - Search for conversation in any place or for last user messages
  • - Return to map view from any place

First of all, we recommend you to register yourself on site and create your profile. From this moment you'll be able to continue or create discussion.

All conversations around the world are placed on a map as a points
. Click any of them to join conversation or just read last messages. Points with new messages will have a different colour:
, also if point is bouncing - new chat message has been arrived to it.

Also messages can contain embeddings, try to send link to youtube video or to sound cloud audio-track - they will be automatically replaced with players.

Enjoy it!
